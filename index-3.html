About Me

I help companies succeed with complex technical challenges and execute successful technology projects through architectural guidance and strategic direction. Whether you're optimizing distributed systems, migrating to cloud platforms, or implementing new technologies, I bring both deep technical expertise and the communication skills to align technical solutions with business objectives.

My approach combines technical architecture with product management principles. I bring a product management mindset that starts with user needs and business objectives, making sure to understand your users, business goals, and market context - not just technical specifications. Some clients need help with complex technical challenges for relatively simple products, while others require more strategic product thinking.

Currently serving as Solutions Architect at Naval Air Warfare Center Weapons Division (NAWCWD), I have over a decade of experience as an independent consultant and previously co-founded Jolt OS, a successful iOS/iPad app development company with multiple featured apps in the Apple App Store.
